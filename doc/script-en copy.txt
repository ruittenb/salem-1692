
A. The night is falling. The town goes to sleep; everybody please close
your eyes.


B. Once all eyes are closed, the witch wakes up.

E. The witch opens her eyes and without making a sound, she decides who
will receive the black cat. She MAY choose herself.

H. The witch has made her choice and closes her eyes.


D. Once all eyes are closed, the witches wake up.  The witches open
their eyes and make eye contact to acknowledge each other.

F. With gestures only and without making a sound, they decide who will
receive the black cat. They MAY choose one of themselves.

G. With gestures only and without making a sound, they decide who they
want to kill. They MAY choose one of themselves.

J. The witches have made their choice and close their eyes.


M. Once the witches have closed their eyes, the constable wakes up.  The
constable decides who will be protected this night.  He may NOT choose
himself.

N. The constable has made his choice and closes his eyes.

Z. The dawn is breaking. The town wakes up, everybody please open your eyes.
